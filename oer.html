Open Educational Resourses (OER)

Open educational resources (OER) are free and openly licensed educational materials that can be used for teaching, learning, research, and other purposes.

Developing OER was a major initiative for MEC during the 2018-2019 academic year. Below we list some of our accomplishments and statistics pertaining to our OER efforts.

OER has been completely implimented in the following courses MAT095, MAT099, MAT117, MAT115, MAT119, MAT120.

The master sections for these courses are in their final versions. And new syllabi have been approved by both the department and college curriculum commiteeies. In addition, in Spring I 2019, we are piloting a section of MAT121 using OER resourses.

Student textbook and online platform savings

For Fall I and II of 2018 there were a total enrollment of more than 5,650 students in 207 OER sections. In Spring 2019 an OER MAT121 section was piloted. For Spring I and II 2019 there were 4,634 students in 159 OER sections. This has led to an estimated textbook savings of $846,907.25 for the 2018-2019 academic year.

OER materials and platforms

For all of our OER sections we are using the Lumen Learning Platform. Our master sections are hosted on their Lumen ohm server. Lumen offers a complete education managment system with grade books, a problem bank and communication capabilities.
